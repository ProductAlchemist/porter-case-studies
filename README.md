# Porter Product Case Studies

A collection of product case studies documenting my work building 0→1 platforms at Porter.

## Overview

This repository showcases detailed case studies of platform products I've built, focusing on problem-solving approach, solution architecture, measurable impact, and key learnings.

## Case Studies

### 1. [Lending Platform (0→1)](./lending-platform.md)
Built and scaled complete lending infrastructure from scratch, achieving 10x growth in platform adoption.

**Key Metrics:**
- 10x growth in adoption
- Enabled credit access for thousands of logistics partners
- Full-stack platform from credit scoring to disbursement

### 2. [Taxation Infrastructure](./taxation-system.md)
Designed tax compliance system handling complex regulatory requirements and enterprise-scale operations.

**Key Metrics:**
- ₹2.4 Cr in cost savings
- Automated compliance for enterprise customers
- Reduced manual processing time significantly

### 3. [Fraud Prevention System](./fraud-prevention.md)
Developed behavioral design-based fraud detection and prevention system protecting platform integrity.

**Key Metrics:**
- Reduced fraud incidents while maintaining user experience
- Protected transactions across the platform
- Behavioral nudges preventing fraudulent behavior

## About These Case Studies

- **Solution**: Product approach, key features, and architecture decisions
- **Impact**: Quantifiable business outcomes and metrics
- **Learnings**: Key insights and takeaways from the product journey
